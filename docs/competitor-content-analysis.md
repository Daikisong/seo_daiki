# Competitor Content Analysis

The analyzer stores structured summaries:

- title pattern
- H1 and headings
- intent served
- content type
- monetization pattern
- comparison table presence
- product links presence
- original data presence
- freshness signals
- strengths and weaknesses
- missing angles

It does not store full article bodies and must not rewrite competitor wording.

Example output:

```text
Top results cover USB-C wattage and price, but most do not explain safety verification for Spain.
```
