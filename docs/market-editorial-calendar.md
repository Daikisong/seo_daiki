# Market Editorial Calendar

Each market has its own queue.

Example:

- US magnesium sleep stays in `/us/en/`.
- Spain USB-C charger stays in `/es/es/`.
- Brazil power bank capacity stays in `/br/pt-br/`.

The calendar avoids publishing too many unrelated trend posts in one market during a short window.

Commands:

```bash
pnpm calendar:build
pnpm calendar:report
```
